# Data Sources

## 1930 Edition

The faalupega data in this project is derived from:

> **O le tusi faalupega o Samoa : Savaii, Upolu, Manono, Ma Apolima.**
> Published 1930.

This work is held by the **National Library of Australia** and is catalogued at:
https://nla.gov.au:443/nla.obj-52422940

### Research & Educational Use

This tool is intended for **research and educational purposes only**. The faalupega data included in this project is used for the purpose of preserving and making accessible traditional Samoan cultural records.

### Cultural Sensitivity

The National Library of Australia states: *"Please respect indigenous cultural and ethical concerns."*

Faalupega are sacred traditional records central to Samoan culture and governance. They should be treated with respect and cultural sensitivity. This tool is intended to support research and education, not to replace or diminish the authority of traditional knowledge holders.
